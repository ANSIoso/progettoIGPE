




Sir.Platinum, a native of Pluto, part of the Platini dynasty chosen as a military leader in the battles of Kraiten and Valsey. 
He is distinguished by his fluidity of movement and excellent use of weapons, especially the sword. 
Legendary is his connection with the Sword of Toiken, entrusted to him by the last king of Pluto before he was killed by the Meidi people. 
In 123 BC Pluto was invaded by the Meidi and they brought death and destruction killing almost the entire population. 
A few people, including Sir.Platinum, managed to survive. 
Before the last king of Pluto died he commissioned Sir. Platinum to kill the entire Meidi population and restore Pluto to its former glory.
